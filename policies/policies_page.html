Policies

On Refunds and Cancellation of Orders

Orders may be cancelled within 24 hours of submitting an order form. Credit Card payments are refundable within that time limit. However, bank transfers cannot be refunded at all.
Orders cannot be cancelled after 24 hours as the work would already be underway.

On Shipment of Orders

Each commission comes with the option of getting a physical print.
Meet-ups can be arranged for those residing in the local area. Local shipping costs will vary depending on the courier service. This can be discussed between the customer and the artist.
International shipping fees will be covered by the customer.

On Payment Methods

The shop is currently new and is only operating digitally. Cash payment cannot be accepted.
